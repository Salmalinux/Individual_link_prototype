How the link works

1. Each member gets a unique personal link.
2. When they open it, they see a confirmation page — they must click "Join" to proceed.
3. Only after clicking "Join" is the link consumed. WhatsApp/email preview scanners just load the page but never click, so they won't use up the link.
4. Once used, the link is permanently expired — sharing it does nothing.
⚠️ Danger zone. Resetting will permanently delete all invite records and links. This cannot be undone. Your WhatsApp group link and settings will be kept.
